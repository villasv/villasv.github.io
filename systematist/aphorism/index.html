Aphorism

An aphorism is a concise, terse, laconic, or memorable expression of a general truth or principle. It’s a useful tool for meditation and serves as easy reminders for important lessons or aspirations that could use a bit more attention.

Examples:

  • Listen To The Sound Of Silence
  • There’s A Language Without Question Marks
