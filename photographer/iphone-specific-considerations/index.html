iPhone Specific Considerations

iPhone cameras lenses do not have movable iris so the Aperture isn’t adjustable. Instead, Apple provides different lenses with combinations of Aperture and Focal Length. For example, the iPhone 13 Pro has:

  • Ultra wide Lens -> 13 mm focal length, f/1.8 aperture
  • Wide Lens -> 26 mm focal length, f/1.5 aperture
  • Telephoto Lens -> 77 mm focal length, f/2.8 aperture

  • Use Panoramic Mode sparingly (when 0.5x lens isn’t enough) and careful with exposure changes
