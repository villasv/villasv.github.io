Editing on Adobe Lightroom

Adobe Lightroom is a popular photo editing application, available on smartphones, tablets and desktop.

Light Adjustments

The most common task is correcting for white balance.

Color Adjustments

  • Temperature controls the emphasis on blue vs orange
  • Tint controls the emphasis on green vs pink

Both adjustments are mainly used for correcting for while balance if the shooting conditions affect the subject.

  • Vibrance controls boosts to colors that are not not already bright
  • Saturation controls boosts to the intensity of all the colors in the photo

In the Mix mode, you can edit individual colors in the photo.

  • Hue controls color shifting
  • Saturation controls boosts to the intensity of this color
  • Luminance controls the brightness of this color

In the Gradient tab it’s possible to perform similar hue adjustments but focusing on shadows, middle tones or highlights at a time.

Effects

  • Texture sharpens or smooths the edges
  • Clarity is similar to texture but coupled with contrast
  • Dehaze controls a fog-like blur
  • Vignette controls the brightness of the corners of the image, used to emphasize the center
  • Midpoint controls the advance of the Vignette
  • Feather controls the transition of the Vignette
  • Roudness controls the excentricity of the vignette

Details

  • Sharpenning can recover a blurry image
  • Radius, Detail and Masking are further advanced controls for Sharpenning
  • Noise Reduction cleans up grains and distorted pixels

Optics

Lens corrections, sometimes useful for wide lens and fixing fish eye effects.
