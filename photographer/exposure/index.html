Exposure

Camera exposure controls the amount of light (per unit area) received by the camera, affecting the image brightness.

The luminous exposure is controlled by two settings: Shutter Speed and Aperture. Changing the Camera ISO also affects the brightness of photos and some apps might tinker with it when adjusting exposure, but it doesn’t technically change the amount of light that enters the camera - just the sensitivity of the sensors inside the camera.
