Inspired by Henry Luce's LIFE: to see life; to see the world; to witness great events; the faces of the poor and the gestures of the proud; to see strange things; to see humankind's work and discoveries; to see things far away, things hidden behind walls and within rooms; things dangerous to come to; to see and take pleasure in seeing; to see and be amazed; to see and be instructed; to draw closer, find each other, and to feel.
