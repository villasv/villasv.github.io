Pour Over

Bypass with frilled filters/drippers.

Rinse out the paper + filter. It will remove paper taste and pre-heat the filter. Add the grounds and create a little well in the middle of the grounds.

Recipes

1:17 ratio (15g : 255g) 30g bloom (2 minutes) All the rest (2 minutes) Swirl

James Hoffman’s V60 Recipe

Bloom with 2:1, getting all the coffee wet. It’s okay to have a bit more, but no more than 3:1. Swirl during bloom as well. Rest it for 30 to 45 seconds. Pour 60% of total target in 30 seconds. That’s enough to give it a good agitation but not too much. After that the cone should be pretty full. Pour the rest in the next 30 seconds. Stir with a spoon a bit for both sides. Allow the water to drawdown a bit and give it a good swirl. Pouring to directly to the coffee helps stirring and makes it run down faster. At the end there should be a flat bed, some fines stuck to the edges but not many large pieces.
