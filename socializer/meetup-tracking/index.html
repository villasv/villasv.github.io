Meetup Tracking

The contact evaluation metric considers three aspects: availability, friendliness, and initiative. Each aspect is graded in the set {1, 2, 3, 4} according to the following criteria:

  • Availability
    1. Requires week-long planning or more
    2. Requires up to a day or two of planning
    3. Planning preferred but doable by end of day
    4. Could happen almost any time without notice
  • Friendliness
    1. Some topics are preferably avoided
    2. Conversations are relaxed by default
    3. Frequently one loses track of time
    4. Tempting to keep going for an all-nighter
  • Initiative
    1. Rarely initiates a conversation
    2. Sometimes initiates a conversation
    3. Frequently hits up for a chat or a meet
    4. Invites for all sorts of activities
